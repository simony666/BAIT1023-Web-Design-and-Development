Hotel Facilities

A sanctuary for adventure and relaxation

Whether your plans include tropical adventure, rejuvenation or relaxation, we offer an array of activities for all. A main swimming pool, pitch-and-putt golf course and more are available for your enjoyment. Dive in or sail away on glittering blue waters, or keep fit with our sports and activities.

Gymnasium
Facilities | Gymnasium

Keep fit on your vacation

Hotel offers a wide range of fully-equipped fitness facilities and a state-of-the-art gymnasium. The male and female changing rooms each offer a sauna, steam room, hot whirlpool bath and relaxation areas for in-house guests. Open from 8:00am to 10:00pm daily.

Outdoor Swimming Pool
Facilities | Outdoor Swimming Pool

For Adult's swimming pool

A stunning 3km white sandy beach, Pantai Dalit offers private expanses of amazingly soft, white sand. Our swimming pool is for a cool dip within luxuriant gardens. Open from 10:00am to 8:00pm daily.

Island Pool
Facilities | Island Pool

For children's swimming pool

Our goal is to ensure a relaxing, happy tropical holiday for you and your entire family. The children's pool and main pool are located in the Garden Wing pool area. Open from 10:00am to 8:00pm daily.

Golf
Facilities | Golf

Tee off with a view

A nine-hole, par-3 pitch-and-putt golf course is extremely well crafted and beautifully landscaped. With holes ranging from 25 to 64 metres, golfers can play with just a pitching wedge and a putter. There is also a practice green and a small reception area to house putters, wedges, balls and tees. Open from 7:00am to 9:00pm daily.

Tennis Courts
Facilities | Tennis Court

Tee off amidst natural beauty

Perfect your game at one of our 6 tennis courts where coaching is available with prior booking. 2 artificial grass and 4 clay courts are available. Open from 7:00am to 9:00pm daily.

Spa
Facilities | Spa

Luxurious spa treatments

A place for guests to live a balanced life during their trip. The Spa offers a selection of treatments that showcase the diverse Sabah culture, with natural botanical and herbal products known for their therapeutic properties. Open from 10:00am to 8:00pm daily.
