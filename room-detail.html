Single Room

  • Size: The room measures about 200 square feet.
  • Bed: The room has one twin-sized bed with clean linens and pillows.
  • Furniture: There is a small desk and chair for working, as well as a nightstand and lamp next to the bed.
  • Entertainment: There is a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a remote control.
  • Climate control: The room has individual heating and air conditioning controls for personal comfort.
  • Lighting: There are multiple light fixtures and lamps for bright or dim lighting options.
  • Bathroom: The room has a private bathroom with a toilet, sink, and shower. Towels, soap, and shampoo are provided.
  • View: Depending on the location of the room, there may be a view of the surrounding city or landscape.
  • Amenities: Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, and there may be a mini-fridge, coffee maker, or other amenities in the room.

Superior Room

  • Size: The superior room is larger than a standard room, measuring about 300-400 square feet.
  • Bed: The room has one king-sized or queen-sized bed with plush linens and pillows.
  • Furniture: In addition to a desk and chair, there may be a comfortable armchair, sofa, or other seating area in the room.
  • Storage: The room has a larger closet or wardrobe with more space for storing clothes and luggage.
  • Entertainment: There is a flat-screen TV with cable channels, as well as a DVD player or other entertainment options.
  • Climate control: The room has individual heating and air conditioning controls for personal comfort, and may have additional ceiling fans or windows for natural ventilation.
  • Lighting: There are multiple light fixtures and lamps for bright or dim lighting options, as well as curtains or blinds for controlling natural light.
  • Bathroom: The room has a private bathroom with a toilet, sink, and shower or bathtub. Towels, bathrobes, and high-end toiletries are provided.
  • View: Depending on the location of the room, there may be a better view of the surrounding city or landscape, and some rooms may have a balcony or terrace.
  • Amenities: Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, and the room may have a mini-fridge, coffee maker, or other amenities such as a safe, iron and ironing board, or additional electrical outlets.

Superior Family Combo Room

  • The superior family combo room is even larger than a standard superior room, measuring around 500-600 square feet, and can comfortably accommodate a family of four or more.
  • Beds: The room typically has two or more beds, including a king-sized or queen-sized bed for parents and twin-sized or bunk beds for children. The beds have plush linens and pillows.
  • Furniture: In addition to a desk and chair, the room may have a sofa bed, armchairs, or other seating areas for the family to relax and socialize.
  • Storage: The room has a larger closet or wardrobe with more space for storing clothes and luggage for the whole family.
  • Entertainment: There are multiple flat-screen TVs with cable channels, as well as a DVD player, game console, or other entertainment options to keep the whole family entertained.
  • Bathroom: The room has a private bathroom with a toilet, sink, and shower or bathtub. Towels, bathrobes, and high-end toiletries are provided for the whole family.
  • Additional amenities: Some superior family combo rooms may also include a mini-fridge, coffee maker, safe, iron and ironing board, or additional electrical outlets. Some hotels may offer additional perks such as a complimentary breakfast, access to a private lounge or pool area, or concierge service.
